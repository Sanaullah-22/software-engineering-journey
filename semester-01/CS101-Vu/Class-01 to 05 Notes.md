
Day 1 – CS101 Notes (Lectures 1–5)

📌 Lecture 1: Introduction to Computer Science

- Computer Science: Study of how computers work and solve problems
- Hardware: Physical components (keyboard, mouse, monitor)
- Algorithms: Step-by-step instructions to complete a task
- Information Processing: Handling and manipulating data (IT)
- Importance: Used in all fields (Business, Engineering, Science)

---

📌 Lecture 2: Learning Strategies

🔹 Breadth-First Learning

- Learn basics of many topics
- Helps understand the big picture

🔹 Depth-First Learning

- Focus deeply on one topic
- Gain mastery before moving on

---

📌 Lecture 3: Search Engine Basics

- Search Engines: Tools to find information (e.g., Google)
- Query: Words typed in search bar
- Case-Insensitive: CAPITAL or small letters don’t matter
- Better Results: Use specific keywords
- HTTPS: Secure websites

---

📌 Lecture 4: Searching Tricks

- Weather → "Weather + city"
- Calculator → "5 * 10", "Subtract 10 from 30"
- Conversions → "USD to PKR", "m to cm"

---

📌 Lecture 5: Search Operators

- "" "" → Exact phrase
- "@" → Social media
- "#" → Hashtags
- "$" → Price
- "*" → Wildcard (missing word)

---

✅ Key Takeaways

- CS is about problem-solving, not just coding
- Algorithms are the core of computing
- Smart searching = faster learning
- Learning strategies help manage complex topics
